## Overview
This project focuses on requirement analysis and system modeling for the **UB Online Learning System**. The platform is designed to facilitate the browsing, purchasing, and management of educational courses and materials, operating as a marketplace that connects Learners (Buyers) with Instructors (Sellers).

## My Role
Business Analyst

## Responsibilities
- **Requirement Analysis:** Elicited and analyzed business requirements for an education marketplace platform, managing interactions between Learners, Instructors, and Administrators.
- **System Modeling:** Designed UML diagrams to visualize the system's structural and behavioral architecture:
  - **Class Diagram:** Defined the data schema including Users, Course Inventory, Order Processing, and Delivery (for physical learning materials).
  - **Sequence Diagram:** Modeled the dynamic flow for user account management (MVC pattern).
- **Process Optimization:** Streamlined workflows for course registration, material dispatching, and user profile management.
- **Documentation:** Created BRD and SRS documents to bridge the gap between business needs and technical implementation.
